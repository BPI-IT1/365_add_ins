Email Impersonation Response

Thanks for bringing this to our attention.

It appears someone is impersonating Barney’s Pumps using a non-affiliated email domain (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo). Please note: all legitimate emails from us will come from @barneyspumps.com.

Unfortunately, while we use email authentication tools (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), we cannot prevent others from sending spoofed messages using unrelated domains.

What you should do:

  • Do not respond to the suspicious email or click any links.
  • Please forward the message to your internal IT or security team for review.
  • Mark the message as phishing in your email client, if possible.
  • If the message involves account or payment information, please call our main office line at (863) 665-8500 to verify.

We follow the NIST Cybersecurity Framework to manage these risks:

  1. IDENTIFY: Recognize threats like spoofing.
  2. PROTECT: Use authentication and awareness training.
  3. DETECT: Monitor for suspicious activity.
  4. RESPOND: Notify and coordinate with affected parties.
  5. RECOVER: Improve controls to reduce future risk.

If you’re ever unsure about a message, feel free to contact us directly at 863-665-8500.

Thanks again for your vigilance.
